Song Popularity Prediction

This group project was completed at the end of ISYE 6414 - Regression Analysis in the Fall 2021 semester. The goal of this project was to create a model to accurately predict the popularity of a song based on several of its attributes stored by Spotify. Different attempted approaches include ordinary least squares regression, weighted least squares regression, LASSO, adding interaction terms, variable transformations with BoxCox, random forest, and catboost.
